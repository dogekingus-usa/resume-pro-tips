How to Answer Why Do You Want to Work Here

May 2026 · 6 min read · 🎤 Interviews

Why This Question Matters

It tests three things: whether you have done research, whether your values align, and how serious you are. A generic answer signals you are applying everywhere. A specific researched answer demonstrates genuine interest.

The Three-Part Answer

The Company (40%): Reference something specific about their product mission or market position. The Role (40%): Connect the role to your skills and career goals. The Fit (20%): Explain why you personally would thrive there referencing culture values or team dynamics.

What Not to Say

Avoid generic answers like I need a job or your company is hiring. Do not focus on compensation. Do not say you like the product without specifics. Show initiative through research.
