# How to Create a Career Portfolio That Lands Jobs

May 2026 · 8 min read · ?? Career

## What Is a Career Portfolio
A career portfolio is a curated collection of your best work that demonstrates your skills and impact more vividly than a resume ever could. It is especially valuable for creative roles tech positions consulting and any role where visual evidence matters.


## What to Include
Case studies of key projects with problem approach and results. Writing samples or content you have produced. Presentations and slide decks. Data visualizations and dashboards. Code repositories or design prototypes. Testimonials and recommendations. Certifications and credentials.


## How to Structure It
Create a simple clean portfolio site or PDF. Lead with your strongest work. For each project provide context your specific contribution and measurable results. Keep it to 5-7 pieces maximum quality over quantity.
