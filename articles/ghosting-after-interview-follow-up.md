# Ghosting After Interview: What to Do and How to Follow Up

May 2026 · 5 min read · ?? Career

## Why Interview Ghosting Happens
Hiring processes get delayed budgets get frozen decision-makers get busy or internal candidates emerge. Understanding why helps you respond strategically rather than emotionally.


## Your Follow-Up Timeline
Day 1-2: Send thank you email immediately. Day 7: Gentle check-in if no response. Day 14: Send a substantive follow-up adding value like a relevant article. Day 21: Send final email and move on.


## What to Do Instead of Waiting
Never stop your job search while waiting for one company. Continue applying networking and interviewing elsewhere. Multiple options give you leverage and reduce emotional impact.
