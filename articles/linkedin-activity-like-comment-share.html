LinkedIn Activity: What to Like Comment and Share

May 2026 · 6 min read · ��™� LinkedIn

Your Activity Is Your Brand

Everything you do on LinkedIn - likes comments shares and posts - contributes to your professional brand. Recruiters and hiring managers often review your activity before reaching out.

What to Like

Like posts that align with your professional values and interests. Avoid controversial or divisive content. Like content from industry leaders companies you admire and peers whose work you respect.

What to Comment

Add value with every comment. Share your perspective ask thoughtful questions or provide additional insights. Avoid generic comments like Great post. Instead say This reminds me of a project where we applied similar principles and saw X result.

What to Share

Share industry news with your take on it. Share your own content and insights. Share others work that you genuinely find valuable. Add context explaining why you are sharing each piece.
