# LinkedIn Content Strategy: Build Your Personal Brand

## Why LinkedIn Content Matters
Posting consistently on LinkedIn establishes you as a thought leader in your field, increases your visibility to recruiters, and builds a professional network that can open doors throughout your career. You don't need to be a full-time content creator ' a strategic, low-effort approach can deliver significant results.


## The 1-Hour-Per-Week Strategy
You can build a strong LinkedIn presence with just one hour per week. Spend 20 minutes curating and commenting on others' content, 20 minutes writing one original post, and 20 minutes engaging with comments on your post. Consistency matters more than volume ' one quality post per week outperforms five rushed posts.


## What to Post About
**Industry Insights:** Share your perspective on industry trends, news, or developments. Add your own take rather than just sharing a link. "Most people think X about [trend], but my experience in [field] has shown me Y."


**Professional Lessons:** Share something you learned the hard way. Vulnerability and authenticity perform well on LinkedIn. "I made a mistake early in my career that taught me a valuable lesson about [topic]. Here's what I learned."


**Career Advice:** Help others in your field with actionable advice. Posts that teach specific skills or strategies consistently get high engagement.


## Content Format Best Practices
- Write a compelling first line ' it determines whether readers click "see more"- Use short paragraphs (1-3 sentences max) for readability- Include a clear takeaway or call to action at the end- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags (no more)- Post when your audience is most active (typically weekday mornings)
