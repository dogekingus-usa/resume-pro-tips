Your Complete LinkedIn Optimization Checklist

An optimized LinkedIn profile significantly increases your visibility to recruiters and hiring managers. Use this comprehensive 25-point checklist to audit every section of your profile and identify opportunities for improvement.

Profile Essentials

1. Professional headshot with clear face, neutral background, and appropriate attire. 2. Custom public profile URL with your name. 3. Background banner image that reinforces your professional brand. 4. Headline includes target role keywords. 5. Location is accurate and relevant.

About and Experience

6. Compelling opening that hooks readers. 7. Clear value proposition in first 3 lines. 8. Keywords naturally integrated. 9. Quantifiable achievements. 10. Call to action at the end. 11. Each role has 3-5 bullet points. 12. Consistent verb tense. 13. Media attachments added. 14. Keywords reflect industry. 15. Career narrative is clear.

Skills and Social Proof

16. Top 3 skills are most marketable. 17. Skill list is curated. 18. At least 5 endorsements for top 3 skills. 19. Skills match headline and about section. 20. Education section is complete. 21. Recommendations from 3+ people. 22. Completed Skill Assessments. 23. Featured section is active. 24. Volunteer experience listed if relevant. 25. Certifications and courses are current.
