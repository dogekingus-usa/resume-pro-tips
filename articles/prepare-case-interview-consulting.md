# How to Prepare for a Case Interview Consulting

May 2026 · 10 min read · ?? Interviews

## What Is a Case Interview
A case interview presents a business problem and asks you to solve it in real time. Consulting firms use them to assess your analytical thinking structured problem-solving and communication skills under pressure.


## Key Frameworks
Profitability Framework: Revenue minus costs examine drivers of each. Market Entry Framework: Market attractiveness competition capabilities timing. Merger and Acquisition Framework: Strategic fit financials integration plan. Each framework provides a starting structure but adapt to the specific case.


## The Structured Approach
Clarify the question. Build a framework. Gather data through questions. Analyze and synthesize. Make a recommendation. Always summarize your logic at the end and provide a clear actionable recommendation.
