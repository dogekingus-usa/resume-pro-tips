# Resume File Format: PDF vs Word Doc in 2026

May 2026 · 5 min read · ?? Resume Tips

## The Great Resume Format Debate
PDF or Word The answer depends on how you submit and what systems process it. In 2026 modern ATS systems handle both well but there are still important differences.


## PDF Pros and Cons
Pros: Preserves formatting perfectly looks professional cannot be easily edited. Cons: Some older ATS systems struggle with PDF parsing especially complex layouts.


## Word Doc Pros and Cons
Pros: Best ATS compatibility easily parsed for keywords preferred by corporate HR. Cons: Formatting can shift less professional appearance can be accidentally edited.


## Best Strategy for 2026
Use PDF for direct company career portal submissions. Use Word for external job boards like LinkedIn and Indeed. When in doubt submit both.
