10 Cover Letter Templates That Get Interviews

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on crafting the perfect cover letter for your job application! A well-crafted cover letter can be a powerful tool in getting you noticed by potential employers. Here, we will explore 10 different cover letter templates designed to fit various scenarios, from standard applications to specific situations like career change or networking.

Standard Cover Letter Template

This is the most common type of cover letter and should be your go-to for general job applications. It's professional, straightforward, and tailored to the position you're applying for.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Employer's Last Name],

        I am writing to apply for the [Position] at [Company], as advertised on [where you found it]. With [X years/months] of experience in [relevant field or skill], and a passion for [related interests or projects], I believe that my skills and background make me an ideal candidate.

        In my current role as [Current Position], I have honed my abilities in [specific skills related to the job description]. My work has been recognized through [mention any awards, promotions, etc.], which demonstrates my commitment to excellence and dedication to continuous improvement.

        At [Company Name], I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my expertise and creativity towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description]. I am confident that my experience and skills would be a valuable asset to your team.

        Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the possibility of discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Career Change Cover Letter Template

When transitioning to a new field, it's crucial to highlight your transferable skills and experiences that are relevant to the job you're applying for. This template is designed to showcase your journey and the value you can bring despite changing careers.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Employer's Last Name],

        I am writing to apply for the [Position] at [Company], as advertised on [where you found it]. With a background in [your previous field or degree], I have developed skills and experiences that are highly relevant to this role.

        In my current position as [Current Position], I have honed my abilities in [specific transferable skills related to the job description]. For instance, my experience in [specific task or project] at [previous company name] has given me a solid foundation in [related skill set].

        As an individual with a passion for [new field of interest], I am excited about the opportunity to bring my unique perspective and transferable skills to [Company Name]. My [relevant experiences, certifications, or education] have prepared me well for this transition, and I am confident that I can make significant contributions to your team.

        Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Entry-Level Cover Letter Template

For entry-level positions, it's essential to emphasize your enthusiasm and willingness to learn. This template is tailored for those just starting their careers or transitioning from a non-related field.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Employer's Last Name],

        I am writing to apply for the [Position] at [Company], as advertised on [where you found it]. With a strong academic background in [your degree or major] and an unwavering passion for [related interests or projects], I believe that my skills, enthusiasm, and eagerness to learn make me an ideal candidate.

        During my time as a student/intern at [Previous Company Name], I gained valuable experience in [specific tasks or projects]. This hands-on learning has equipped me with the foundational knowledge necessary to excel in this role. My commitment to continuous improvement and dedication to excellence have prepared me well for the challenges that lie ahead.

        At [Company Name], I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my energy, creativity, and willingness to learn towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description]. I am confident that I can bring a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to your team.

        Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Referral Cover Letter Template

A referral cover letter leverages the trust and credibility of an internal connection. This template helps you capitalize on this advantage by expressing your gratitude for the introduction and emphasizing why you are a good fit.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Referrer's Last Name],

        I am writing to apply for the [Position] at [Company], as referred by my colleague [Referrer's Name]. With a background in [your previous field or degree], and an enthusiasm for [related interests or projects], I believe that my skills, experience, and dedication make me an excellent candidate.

        In my current position as [Current Position], I have honed my abilities in [specific transferable skills related to the job description]. My work has been recognized through [mention any awards, promotions, etc.], which demonstrates my commitment to excellence and dedication to continuous improvement.

        Your recommendation means a great deal to me, and it is an honor to be considered for this role at [Company Name]. I am excited about the opportunity to bring my expertise and creativity towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description]. My experience has prepared me well for this transition, and I am confident that I can make significant contributions to your team.

        Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Cold Application Cover Letter Template

A cold application letter is sent to a company where you are not currently known. This template helps you introduce yourself and express your interest in joining their team.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Employer's Last Name],

        I am writing to express my interest in the [Position] at [Company], which I recently learned about through [where you found it]. With a strong background in [your previous field or degree] and an unwavering passion for [related interests or projects], I believe that my skills, enthusiasm, and dedication make me an ideal candidate.

        In my current position as [Current Position], I have honed my abilities in [specific transferable skills related to the job description]. My work has been recognized through [mention any awards, promotions, etc.], which demonstrates my commitment to excellence and dedication to continuous improvement.

        Your company's reputation for [mention specific aspects of the company you admire] resonates with me, and I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my energy, creativity, and willingness to learn towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description]. My experience has prepared me well for this transition, and I am confident that I can bring a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to your team.

        Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Follow-Up Cover Letter Template

A follow-up letter is sent after an interview or when you have not heard back from a company. This template allows you to reiterate your interest and provide any additional information that might be useful.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Employer's Last Name],

        I am writing to follow up on my application for the [Position] at [Company], which I submitted on [Date of Application]. As we discussed in our recent interview, I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my skills and experience towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description].

        Since our conversation, I have had the chance to reflect further on how my background in [your previous field or degree] aligns with the needs of your team. My work has been recognized through [mention any awards, promotions, etc.], which demonstrates my commitment to excellence and dedication to continuous improvement.

        Your company's reputation for [mention specific aspects of the company you admire] resonates with me, and I am eager to bring a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to your team. If there is any additional information or documentation that would be beneficial at this stage, please do not hesitate to let me know.

        Thank you once again for considering my application. I look forward to the possibility of discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

Networking Cover Letter Template

A networking cover letter is sent to someone you know personally or professionally, asking for an introduction or a referral. This template allows you to leverage your connections and express your gratitude.


        [Your Name]  
        [Your Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]  
        [Email Address]  
        [Phone Number]  
        [Date]

        [Employer's Name]  
        [Company's Name]  
        [Company's Address]  
        [City, State, ZIP Code]

        Dear [Name of Referrer],

        I am writing to express my gratitude for your recent introduction and to inquire about the possibility of applying for the [Position] at [Company]. Your recommendation means a great deal to me, and it is an honor to be considered for this role.

        With a background in [your previous field or degree], and an enthusiasm for [related interests or projects], I believe that my skills, experience, and dedication make me an ideal candidate. In my current position as [Current Position], I have honed my abilities in [specific transferable skills related to the job description]. My work has been recognized through [mention any awards, promotions, etc.], which demonstrates my commitment to excellence and dedication to continuous improvement.

        Your company's reputation for [mention specific aspects of the company you admire] resonates with me, and I am excited about the opportunity to contribute my energy, creativity, and willingness to learn towards [specific goals or projects mentioned in the job description]. My experience has prepared me well for this transition, and I am confident that I can bring a fresh perspective and a strong work ethic to your team.

        If there is any additional information or documentation that would be beneficial at this stage, please do not hesitate to let me know. Thank you once again for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s success further.

        Sincerely,
        [Your Full Name]

These cover letter templates are designed to help you tailor your application process and increase your chances of getting noticed by potential employers. Remember, while these templates provide a solid foundation, it's important to customize each one for the specific job and company to which you are applying. Good luck with your job search!
