The ATS Resume Checklist

20 proven checks to get your resume past robot screeners and into human hands

Check each box. Miss 3+ and your resume gets auto-rejected by most ATS.
Format & File Type
.docx or PDF ATS-friendly. Never .png/.jpg (zero parsability).
Standard fonts Arial, Calibri, Roboto. Fancy fonts = unreadable scan artifacts.
No columns or tables ATS reads leftright, topbottom. Multi-column = scrambled.
No headers/footers Text in them is invisible to 90% of ATS engines. Put contact info in body.
Single-column layout One continuous vertical flow. Sidebars get ignored.
Keywords & Content
Job description keywords Mirror the JD's top 10 skills & tools verbatim.
Standard section headers "Experience", "Education", "Skills". No "My Journey" ATS won't find it.
Action verbs Led, Built, Optimized, Designed, Negotiated. (not: "Was responsible for")
Quantified results "Increased sales 34%", not "Increased sales". Numbers boost ranking.
Industry acronyms Include full + acronym: "Search Engine Optimization (SEO)". Both get matched.
Clean Up
No graphics/logos Icons, progress bars, headshots confuse parsers auto-reject.
1015 pt font Smaller = unreadable scan. Larger = artifacting.
No text boxes or WordArt ATS can't extract text from embedded objects.
Consistent date format "Jan 2020 Mar 2023" throughout. Mixed formats confuse parsers.
Contact info in body No header/footer. Name, phone, email, LinkedIn, location top of page.
Final Sanity Checks
1 page (or 2 max) 10+ years exp = 2 pages. No more. ATS truncates past ~2.5.
Run through Jobscan / Skillroads Match rate should be 80%. If not, rework.
Plain-text test Paste your resume into Notepad. Anything garbled? Fix it in the original.
No personal photos Bias liability. Most ATS auto-strip them; some reject.
EXTRA CRITICAL: Save as "FirstName_LastName_Resume.pdf" never "Resume_FINAL_v3(2).pdf".
Pro Tip: Submit your resume as plain .docx (~20% higher ATS passthrough than PDF). But if the JD says "PDF only" always follow the instruction.
